Associations

           
               

Catholic Library Association – Archives Section

               

Upholds traditional views of historical continuity, ecclesial authority, and the sacredness of record preservation.

                                                               
           
               

Association of Christian Librarians – Archivists and Special Collections Group

               

Though mainly a library organization, this group fosters archival work within Christian higher education and theological institutions. Emphasis on stewardship, truthfulness in historical preservation, and resistance to ideological reinterpretation of records.
